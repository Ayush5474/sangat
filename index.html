Our Story

SANGAT is a vibrant community of history enthusiasts, storytellers, and explorers dedicated to uncovering and sharing the rich heritage of Nashik. We believe that every street, monument, and artifact has a story waiting to be told.

Through our curated heritage walks, we take you on journeys through time, exploring ancient temples, colonial architecture, and hidden historical gems. Our walks are more than just tours - they're immersive experiences where history comes alive.

We also produce engaging content through our blog, YouTube channel, and podcast, making historical knowledge accessible and exciting for everyone.
